About Our Gym

Welcome to the Pimpri Chinchwad Polytechnic Gym! Our gym facility is equipped with state-of-the-art fitness equipment to help you achieve your fitness goals. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced gym-goer, our friendly staff are here to assist you in your fitness journey.

Gym Facilities

  • Cardio machines
  • Strength training equipment
  • Group exercise classes
  • Personal training sessions
  • Locker rooms and showers

Operating Hours

Gym is open from Monday to Saturday, from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays.
