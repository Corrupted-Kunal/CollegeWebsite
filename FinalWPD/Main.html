About Us

The institute was established in the year 1990. It consists of the best Diploma engineering college, spacious buildings for various Engineering Departments, sports ground, canteen, and mess which are spread over a large area of land under Pimpri Chinchwad New Town Development Authority popularly known as Pradhikaran. The plot has been developed into a beautiful landscape in the vicinity of Akurdi railway station.

Akurdi Station is about 20 kilometers away from the Pune Railway Station on the Pune-Mumbai Railway line. The Institute is at a walkable distance from Akurdi railway station. The PC Polytechnic is one of the top Diploma engineering colleges in Pune, it is very well connected by bus routes with many parts of Pune City. Rickshaw and buses ply on roads from Nigdi-Bus stand on Pune-Mumbai high-way to Akurdi station.
