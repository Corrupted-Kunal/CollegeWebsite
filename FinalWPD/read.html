Reading Hall Facility

About the Reading Hall

The reading hall at Pimpri Chinchwad Polytechnic College provides a quiet and conducive environment for students to study and research. It offers a collection of books, journals, and reference materials to support academic pursuits.

Operating Hours

The reading hall is open during the following hours:

  • Monday to Friday: 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM
  • Saturday: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
  • Sunday: Closed

Location

The reading hall is located on the campus of Pimpri Chinchwad Polytechnic College.
